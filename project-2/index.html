What is Ableton?

Ableton is a program where you can edit audio files and arrange them however you want. These programs are called DAWs or Digital Audio Workstation They provide music producers ways to make music without ever having to have an intrument.
If you want you can also plug in almost any instrument into your computer as lomg as you have the right adapters/dongles.

The Basiscs of Ableton

Ableton is a pretty tough program to figure out when you intitially look at because everything is so small and the interface is super big. Ableton is mainly used for live performances which are concerts where DJs stand at a table and have their computer plugged into a bunch of different mechanisms, intruments, and devices.

Ableton can also be used for arragements. This is what people use for putting all of their recordings into different places so that they can make a song out of it. You then export the song and it normalizes the song which means that it makes it so the song doesn't go over the level of volume that that makes it sounds distorted.

Conclusions and Cool Stuff

With Ableton you can do so much, the possibilities are endless, you just have to know how to use all of the different elements properly. If you want to give it a try there is a lite version that is relatively cheap since the whole full version is upwards of $300.
